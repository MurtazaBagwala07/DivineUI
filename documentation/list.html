Lists

Lists can be used at many places wherever multiple items having same style have to be shown.

Example of Unordered Lists

List items of unordered lists are shown via help of bullets. Bullets can be of different shape , we prove classes like 'ul-square-list' for square bullets , 'ul-circle-list' for circular bullets and 'ul-disc-list' for disc shaped bullets.

Example of Ordered List

Ordered List have a parameter to order the list items. We provided classes like 'ol-lower-aplha-list' for small aplhabets, 'ol-upper-aplha-list' for capital aplhabets, 'ol-lower-roman-list' for small roman numbers, 'ol-upper-roman-list' for capital roman numbers.
